Persuasion

What is persuasion?

Is the ability to convince someone to do what you tell them to do

Example of persuasion

A group of people want to play basketball. One kid does not want to play basketball.

One kid says, you should play basketball with us because we need another person.

That kid then goes with them to play basketball.

How do you persuade people?

Step 1: Tell them what they should do

Ex: You should _________

- help me with my homework.

- go play basketball with us.

- buy a new computer.

Step 2: Tell them why they should listen to you

You should help me with my homework because _____

- we are friends.

- the faster I finish it, the sooner we can leave.

- if you do not help me, I will hit you.

Cont.

You should play basketball with us because _________

- it will be fun.

- we need an extra person.

- I want you to join us.

Cont.

You should buy a new computer because _________

- you need it for school.

- they are on sale.

- you can't play games on your current computer.
