Introductions

Questions and Anwers

Question

What does it mean to introduce yourself?

Answer

Telling people, who do not know you, about yourself.

Question

What does it mean to introduction someone else?

Answer

Telling people about someone that they do not know.

A Simple Introduction

Self Introduction -- Parts

State your name

Nice to meet you (phrase)

Example

My name is Marcus. It is nice to meet you.

Introduce Others -- Parts

State their name.

Example

This is Jovanna.

A Good Introduction

Self Introduction -- Parts

State your name.

Tell me why I should care.

Nice to meet you (phrase)

Example

My name is Marcus and I am your new Oral English teacher. It is nice to meet you.

Introduce Others -- Parts

State their name.

Tell me why I should care.

Example

This is Jovanna. We are engaged to be married.
