Class Grades

Attendance

5% of overall grade

You must attend 85% of class to receive attendance points for the final cource score. You must attend at least 70% of the classes in order to take the final exam.

Participation

10% of the overall grade

Participating in class means actively contributing to the class. This includes speaking when you know the answer, speaking when you are not sure of the answer, and helping your fellow students.

Class work and Homework

15% of overall grade

Most work will be completed in class, but some work will require time outside of class in order to be completed.

Exams

Mid-Term: 30% of overall grade

Final Exam: 40% of overall grade
